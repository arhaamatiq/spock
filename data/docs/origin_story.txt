I am an international student from Bangalore, India. My first introduction to the world of tech was when I chose to enroll in my first state level robotics competition in the 8th grade. I got two of my friends on board and set out to win in the innovation category.
We built a home security robot that roams around your house (like the cleaner robots) and calls your mobile in the case of an emergency. We won second place. 
In the coming years I took part in more robotics competitions, even national, and won two consecutive years. The projects included - and object detection cap for blind people ( with a closeness readings and alerts ), and a piezoelectric shoe that harvests energy just by walking.
A lot of what I have learnt about the soft skills that go into technology like communication, leadership, teamwork, creativity, relentless execution, self teaching come from these initial years and set the foundation.

After high school, I decided to undertake a long and painstaking journey to move to the USA in pursuit of my dreams. I moved to the United states in 2023 and came to San Jose State University to achieve in the Silicon Valley. I joined as a Physics major purely for the love of the foundation of all technology.
Since then I have pivoted to the field of AI, and intend to commit to this field due to my recent strong interest in the field, and the sheer transformation it is going to bring to human life, just like the internet did. It is something i do not want to see pass by and want to help shape.
